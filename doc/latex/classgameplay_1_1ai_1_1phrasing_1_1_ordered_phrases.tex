\subsection{Detailed Description}
Class used to contain an ordered list of phrases. 

It is ordered according to the last phrases that were spoken by the \hyperlink{classgameplay_1_1ai_1_1_a_i}{A\-I} 

The last phrases that were spoken by the \hyperlink{classgameplay_1_1ai_1_1_a_i}{A\-I} are pushed at the bottom of the list
