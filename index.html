Philophobia

A new kind of sadistic game

Gameplay

There is a simple guy, wandering for whatever reason(s). He met a strange conscious robot which/who seems to have power over the environment. During his journey, forced to carry the robot, which/who gives him constant orders, hindering his freedom of choice. This is your choice to do what it/he wants or to pick your own choices if you dare to stand up against his omniscient powers. You win if and only if you achieve to regain your liberties. Good luck!
